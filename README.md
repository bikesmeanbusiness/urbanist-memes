# Urbanist Memes

Urbanist Memes is a lightweight website for collecting, browsing, and celebrating pro-city memes, videos, and social snippets. The MVP focuses on:

- **Curated gallery** – A responsive grid of images, short clips, and embeds that highlight inspiring transit, density, and public-space stories.
- **Fast search & filters** – Client-side keyword search plus tag chips (e.g., `transit`, `housing`, `streets`) to help visitors find relevant narratives quickly.
- **Capture & attribution** – Each meme surfaces the original source, links to further reading, and encourages proper credit when reshared.
- **Easy submissions** – A hosted form lets contributors propose new content with context and attribution so moderators can review and publish updates.

Longer term, the site can layer in smarter discovery (semantic search, related content clusters) and community tools, but the initial release prioritizes a simple, reliable browsing experience that works well on phones and shares nicely on social platforms.
